Keybinds and commands

For job specific commands, see above(click on the job)

For typed commands, open the chat window with 't'

  • General Commands/ Keybinds

    P- Open Map
    K- Open Inventory
    F1- Open General/Job menu
    M- Open Phone
    Z- Change voice chat range
    Tab- Ragdoll Emote
    X- Open emote menu
    `-Saved emote button
    T- Open command window
    /carry- Toggle pick player up
    /ooc- Type to server (out of character)
    /job- Find player current job
    /id- Find player  ID
    For Guns/items: place in one of the top inventory slots.  Ammo in another.  Press this button to draw/holster your weapon.  Reload with the button for ammo.

  • Vehicle

    /engine - toggle car engine
    H- hotwire vehicle (faster with tools)
    Q- radio control
    B- put on seatbelt (cant leave car until taken off)
    K- (near trunk) Use trunk inventory
    USE REPAIR KITS TO FIX BROKEN DOWN CARS

  • Miscellaneous/ Other

    /binds- set binds for f2-f11 keys. useful for certain jobs or emotes (e/ handsup, etc)
    U- Open list of players online
